CS Shield Privacy Policy

CS Shield is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not store, process, or retain any customer details, contact submission content, or any personally identifiable information (PII) from users or their customers.

The only information we keep is a simple count of how many contact submissions have been processed by the app. This count is used solely for operational and statistical purposes and cannot be linked to any individual, customer, or submission.

No names, email addresses, message content, or any other data from submissions are ever saved or logged by CS Shield.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support channels provided in the app.
